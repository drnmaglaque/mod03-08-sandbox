    Lantern Restaurant: A Review

    Published by Darren Maglaque | October 20, 2019

    Atmosphere

    A little has gone a long way in this casually good-looking dining room with a long communal table in the middle and a trio of kayaks stowed below the ceiling. Service is speedy and helpful right off the bat, but may evaporate later in the meal.

    Recommended Dishes

    Harbin sausage; spicy and sour cucumbers in garlic sauce; skewered chives; boiled crayfish; boiled lobster; boiled snow crab; spicy wok-fried clams. $1.50 to $3.50; cold appetizers, $4.95 to $12.95; boiled seafood, $15 and up.

    Drinks and Wine
